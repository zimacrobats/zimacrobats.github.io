Our Story

Founded in the heart of Zimbabwe, ZIM Acrobats has brought the vibrant spirit of African performance to global audiences for over two decades. Our troupe consists of exceptionally talented artists dedicated to mastering the art of acrobatics.

We blend traditional Zimbabwean cultural elements with contemporary acrobatic techniques, creating performances that are both authentic and innovative. Each show tells a story, celebrates our heritage, and showcases the incredible athletic ability of our performers.

From intimate venues to international festivals, we've performed across continents, earning acclaim for our unique style, energy, and professionalism.
